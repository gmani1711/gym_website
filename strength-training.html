Benefits of Strength Training

Increased Muscle Mass

Regular strength training stimulates muscle growth, increasing overall muscle mass and improving body composition.

Higher Metabolism

More muscle means a higher resting metabolic rate, helping you burn more calories even when you're not exercising.

Stronger Bones

Weight-bearing exercises increase bone density, reducing the risk of osteoporosis and fractures as you age.
